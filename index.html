About Mumford & Sons

Mumford & Sons is a british rock band from London. The band was formed in 2007 and consists of Marcus Mumford, Ben Lovett, Winston Marshall, and Ted Dwane. Mumford & Sons has three albums: Sigh No More (2009), Babel (2012), and Wilder Mind (2015). The group has been nominated for 14 Grammy nominations and has won the Grammy Award for Album of the Year (Babel). (Source: Wikipedia)

Mumford & Sons music has a folky feel to it and lead singer Marcus Mumford's husky voice adds a unique sound. Their latest album Wilder Mind, has a faster and electric beat too it compared to their previous albums that featured more acoustic tracks.
